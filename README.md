# Simple Pixel

![Simple Pixel Banner](assets/images/README_banner.png)

A lightweight Magisk module designed to bring a Pixel-like experience to your device in a simple and clean way.

---

## 📌 Overview

Simple Pixel is a Magisk module designed to bring a Pixel-like experience to your device in a simple and lightweight way.

It works by promoting Google apps to system-level apps, improving their integration with the system and making them behave more like native system components. In some cases, it may also enable or prepare additional Pixel-like features depending on device compatibility.

The main goal is to transform your phone into a "Mini Pixel" experience — simple, clean, and closer to stock Pixel behavior, without unnecessary complexity.

---

## 📌 Features
- Promotes Google apps to system-level apps
- Better system integration for Google services
- Pixel-like behavior improvements (device dependent)
- Lightweight and systemless via Magisk
- Focus on simplicity and stability

---

## 📱 Supported Architecture
- arm64-v8a

---

## ⚙️ Requirements
- Magisk (latest recommended version)
- Android 8.0 or higher

---

## 📦 Installation
1. Download the latest module ZIP
2. Open the Magisk app
3. Go to **Modules**
4. Tap **Install from storage**
5. Select the Simple Pixel ZIP
6. Reboot your device

---

## ⚠️ Disclaimer
This module modifies system behavior using Magisk systemless methods.

Use at your own risk. The developer is not responsible for bootloops, instability, or any device issues.

Always make a full backup before installing.
